# Smartphone User Research Case Study

## Context
12 interview transcripts with smartphone users aged 25-40 in China and US markets

## Key JTBDs Discovered

### JTBD 1: Capture professional moments effortlessly
When: Traveling or at social events
I want to: Take photos that look professional without editing
So I can: Share memorable moments confidently

### JTBD 2: Stay connected without interruption
When: During work or focused time
I want to: Stay reachable without constant notification distraction
So I can: Maintain focus while still being available for urgent matters

## Key Findings
- Users express significant frustration with photo editing (avg 10+ min/session)
- Notification anxiety is a cross-market pattern
- Battery anxiety drives accessory purchases
- Switching cost (iMessage/ecosystem) is primary inertia force

## Opportunities
1. Auto-enhanced photography (Core, Score: 7.4)
2. Smart notification management (Core, Score: 7.1)
3. Battery optimization transparency (Adjacent, Score: 6.2)
