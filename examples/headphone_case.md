# Wireless Headphone Case Study

## Context
8 switch interviews with users who switched from wired to wireless headphones

## Switch Analysis

### Push Factors
- Wires tangling during commute
- Port plug wearing out on phone
- Being tethered to device

### Pull Factors
- Freedom of movement
- Minimalist aesthetic
- New phone lacking headphone jack

### Inertia Factors
- Concern about Bluetooth quality
- Having to charge another device
- Cost of good wireless headphones

### Anxiety Factors
- Losing small earbuds
- Battery dying mid-use
- Latency for gaming/video

## Key JTBDs
1. When commuting, I want to listen to audio without physical constraints, so I can move freely
2. When working out, I want music that stays in place, so I can focus on exercise

## Insights
- Pull > Push for early adopters
- Battery anxiety is primary adoption barrier
- Sound quality concern is resolved quickly after trying
