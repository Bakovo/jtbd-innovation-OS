# JTBD Core Concepts

## What is a Job?

A job is the progress a person wants to make in a particular situation. Jobs are functional, social, and emotional.

## Core Principles

1. People hire products to get a job done
2. Jobs are stable over time — solutions come and go
3. Focus on the job, not the product
4. Understand the context: when, where, who, before, after

## Job Types

- **Functional Job**: The practical task (e.g., make a hole in the wall)
- **Social Job**: How others perceive the person (e.g., look handy to friends)
- **Emotional Job**: How the person feels about themselves (e.g., feel capable)

## Key Distinction

Feature != Job. A feature is how a solution works. A job is what a person wants to accomplish.

Example:
- Feature: Camera with portrait mode
- Job: Capture memories that feel professional
