# STAR Method for User Interviews

## Framework

- **Situation**: What was the context/background?
- **Task**: What was the goal you were trying to achieve?
- **Action**: What did you actually do?
- **Result**: What happened as a result?

## Application in JTBD

Use STAR to extract concrete user stories:
- Situation -> Context (When)
- Task -> Motivation (I want to)
- Result -> Expected Outcome (So I can)

## Example

S: My phone battery died during a business meeting
T: I needed to share the presentation with the client
A: I borrowed a colleague's laptop and emailed it
R: It worked but was embarrassing and slow
