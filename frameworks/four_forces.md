# Four Forces of Progress

## The Core Framework

Progress = Push + Pull - Inertia - Anxiety

## The Four Forces

### Push (Dissatisfaction with current)
- Frustrations with current solution
- Pain points in current workflow
- Inconveniences that accumulate
- Source: user's current situation problems

### Pull (Attraction of new)
- Appeal of a better solution
- Desired outcomes
- Aspirations and goals
- Source: user's desired future vision

### Inertia (Habit + Status Quo)
- Switching costs (time, money, effort)
- Learning curve
- Emotional attachment to old ways
- Source: reasons user hasn't changed yet

### Anxiety (Fear of change)
- Risk of unknown
- Fear of making wrong choice
- Uncertainty about new solution
- Source: concerns about switching

## Application

When analyzing switches, identify all four forces. Innovation opportunities exist where:
- Push is high
- Anxiety is addressable
- Inertia can be lowered
