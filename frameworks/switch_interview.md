# Switch Interview Method

## Purpose

Understand why a person changed from one solution to another.

## Key Insight

The moment of switch reveals more about user needs than steady-state usage.

## Interview Flow

1. **The Before State**: What was the old solution? Why did you start using it?
2. **The Trigger**: What happened that made you question it?
3. **The Search**: How did you look for alternatives?
4. **The Evaluation**: How did you compare options?
5. **The Switch**: What made you finally decide?
6. **The After**: How is the new solution different?

## What to Capture

- Push factors (dissatisfaction with old solution)
- Pull factors (attraction of new solution)
- Inertia factors (why didn't they switch earlier?)
- Anxiety factors (what worried them about switching?)
