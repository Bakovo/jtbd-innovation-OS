# Task Interview Method

## Purpose

Understand how a user currently accomplishes a job when there is no switch behavior.

## Interview Flow

1. **Last Time**: When was the last time you did X?
2. **Step-by-Step**: Walk me through exactly what you did.
3. **Frustrations**: What was annoying or difficult?
4. **Workarounds**: What did you do instead of the ideal solution?
5. **Perfect World**: If you could wave a magic wand, what would happen?

## Principles

- Focus on specific instances, not generalizations
- Ask about the process, not opinions
- Probe for emotional responses
- Notice what users do, not what they say they do
