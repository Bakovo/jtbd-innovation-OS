# Four Forces Framework

## Push (Motivation Away)
Sources: Current solution frustrations, limitations, inconveniences
Questions: What bugs you about your current solution? What can't you do?

## Pull (Motivation Toward)
Sources: Aspirations, visible benefits of alternatives, word of mouth
Questions: What did the new solution promise? What seemed better?

## Inertia (Resistance to Change)
Sources: Habit, learning cost, switching cost, social pressure
Questions: Why didn't you switch earlier? What kept you with the old solution?

## Anxiety (Fear of Change)
Sources: Risk of unknown, fear of mistakes, uncertainty
Questions: What worried you about switching? What could go wrong?
