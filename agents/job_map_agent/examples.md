# Job Map Examples

Job: Planning a vacation

1. Define: Decide destination, dates, budget
2. Locate: Search for flights, hotels, activities
3. Prepare: Book tickets, pack bags, arrange pet care
4. Confirm: Check all bookings, confirm dates
5. Execute: Travel, stay at hotel, do activities
6. Monitor: Check weather, track spending, watch time
7. Modify: Change plans due to weather, rebook missed flight
8. Conclude: Return home, unpack, share photos
