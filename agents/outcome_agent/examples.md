# Outcome Agent Examples

Need: Users frustrated with comparing insurance plans

Outcome 1: 
- Direction: Minimize
- Object: Time spent comparing plan details
- Context: When evaluating health insurance options
- Metric: Minutes from start to confident decision
- Full: Minimize the time spent comparing health insurance plan details so users can make a confident decision in under 15 minutes

Outcome 2:
- Direction: Eliminate
- Object: Confusion about hidden fees
- Context: When reviewing insurance policy terms
- Metric: Number of surprise fees discovered after signup
- Full: Eliminate confusion about hidden fees so users understand total costs before committing
