# Data Quality Agent Examples

## Example 1: Interview Transcripts

Input: 12 interview transcripts with smartphone users aged 25-40
Report:
- Source: Interview transcripts (1:1 depth interviews)
- Sample: 12 users, 6 female/6 male, ages 25-40
- Time: July 2025
- Risks: Self-report bias possible; moderator may have led questions
- Confidence: High
- Recommendation: Clean 2 transcripts with leading questions; use remaining 10

## Example 2: Survey Data

Input: 500 survey responses with Likert scale questions
Report:
- Source: Online survey
- Sample: 500 responses, 320 complete
- Time: Aug 2025
- Risks: 180 incomplete responses (exclude); possible selection bias (online panel)
- Confidence: Medium
- Recommendation: Use 320 complete responses; cross-validate with qualitative data
