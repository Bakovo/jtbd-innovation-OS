# GTM Framework

## Positioning Formula

For [target user]
Who [JTBD description]
Our product is [category/approach]
That provides [key outcome]
Unlike [alternative]
We [unique difference]

## Channel Selection

- Reach users where they experience the job
- Match channel to context (work vs personal)
- Consider social influence on decision
- Test channels with target persona

## Launch Phases

Phase 1: Early access with power users (validate)
Phase 2: Beta with broader target (learn)
Phase 3: Public launch (scale)
