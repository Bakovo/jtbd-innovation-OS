# Roadmap Framework

## Horizon Definitions

Horizon 1 (Now):
- Core opportunities (score >= 7.0)
- Validated concepts
- Minimum viable job completion

Horizon 2 (Next):
- Adjacent opportunities (score 5.0-6.9)
- Concepts in validation
- Enhanced job completion

Horizon 3 (Future):
- Exploratory opportunities
- Research initiatives
- New job discovery

## Epic Definition Rules

An epic should:
- Address a complete JTBD
- Be deliverable in 1-3 sprints
- Have clear success metrics
- Trace to evidence
